WHAT SEVENT IS

One request. One platform. Full audit trail.

Sevent replaces the old "tender" pattern in the Saudi event industry. An organizer sends one request; the platform routes it to the best 3–5 verified suppliers and tracks every step from request through contract. Payments stay off-platform in the pilot — the system records state and generates a legally-consistent contract.

Organizer

Posts events, sends RFQs, compares quotes, accepts one, reviews the supplier afterwards.

Supplier

Gets verified, publishes catalog + pricing, receives RFQs, sends quotes, confirms bookings.

Admin

Verifies suppliers, resolves disputes, has read-only oversight of the entire system.

Public visitor

Browses approved suppliers by category and city — no login required.

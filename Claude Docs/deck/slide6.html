BUILT-IN CONTROLS

What the platform guarantees

Rules enforced by the system itself — not by procedure, not by user trust.

Role separation

Organizer cannot reach supplier or admin areas, and vice versa. Enforced on every screen.

No self-approval

Only an admin can change a supplier's verified status. Suppliers cannot flip their own state.

No double-booking

A slot cannot be booked twice. Acceptance transaction locks the slot or fails cleanly.

Immutable quotes

Once a quote is sent it cannot be silently edited. Every version is snapshotted with a hash.

Contract equals quote

Contract PDFs are generated from the accepted quote — they cannot disagree.

Cross-supplier privacy

Supplier A cannot read Supplier B's RFQs, documents, or calendar. Verified by test.
